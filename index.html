About

For nearly 40 years, I lived life my own way — broken, restless, and carrying more than I knew how to handle. Everything began to change when I put God at the forefront of my life, my marriage, and my music.

ShadoFaith represents the journey out of the shadows and into faith. The struggles, the healing, the growth, and the moments of victory. My music is honest and comes in many forms — rap, EDM, worship — depending on the season and the message.

Writing music is therapy for my soul. If it helps even one person heal, reflect, or feel less alone, then it’s worth it.
