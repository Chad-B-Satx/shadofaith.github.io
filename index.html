About

I was a messed up person for nearly 40 years until I started putting God in my life — and I still put Him at the front of my life, my marriage, and my music every day.

My music is a representation of my life: the struggles, the growth, and the highlights. I make Christian music in different styles — so you might hear a rap song, then an EDM track, then a worship song. It just depends on the mood and what we feel like creating.

I’ve got a lot of songs that need organizing, mastering, and uploading — time is the main battle — but I’m staying consistent with one purpose: to spread God’s word and help people get healed. Sometimes these songs are my own medicine. Writing lyrics is therapy for my soul.

If any track helps you, please share it with someone who might need it. I know first-hand that God can pull you out of whatever you’ve been through and change your life.
